Artist Bio

Kanishka is an NYC-based conceptual artist and writer, who works with non-human biological systems to further her research in feminist-futurism. She is pursuing a Master of Fine Arts in Photography at Parsons School of Design, where she is developing her practice to produce multimedia interdisciplinary work.
